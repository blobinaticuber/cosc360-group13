# COSC 360 Group 13 Team Contract

## Meeting Details
Meetings will be on Thursdays at 6:30PM in the library. If a member of the group is unable to attend, it is expected that they will notify the group beforehand. In such an event, a brief summary of the meeting will be sent to them afterwards.

## Team Participation
The means of communication will be our group discord server. See the Group 13 announcements page in Canvas for the link to join.

If a group member fails to communicate for an extended time, does not meet deadlines for completing parts of the project that they agreed to complete, or otherwise fails to participate, the other group members can agree to give them a warning. If that member has already been given two warnings, the lab TA or professor will be notified.

## Plagiarism Policy
If it is discovered that a group member has plagiarized any graded work, we will notify the professor or the lab TA. If any plagiarized work is included in the project’s code, it will be removed.

## Conflict Resolution
If a team member is being disrespectful, the other group members should decide if we should confront them and give them a warning, or in severe cases escalate to the professor or lab TA.

## Individual Responsibilities
Individual responsibilities will be fully fleshed out when we start working on the project. Right now it is too early to decide who will work on what, when we are not sure which topic our project is going to be based on.

## Revision Policy
In any meeting following a major assignment, the group will make revisions to the group contract, unless it is agreed that revisions aren’t needed at that time.
